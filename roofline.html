Fascias & Soffits

Fascia boards run along the lower edge of your roof, supporting the bottom row of tiles and carrying all the guttering. Soffits are fitted beneath the fascias, closing off the gap between the wall and the roofline.

We supply and install uPVC fascias and soffits in a range of colours and styles to complement your property. Our products are designed to withstand the British weather without warping, cracking or fading.

  • White, black, anthracite grey and woodgrain finishes
  • Ventilated soffits for roof space airflow
  • Rotten timber replacement included
  • Clean, professional finish guaranteed

Gutters & Downpipes

Efficient guttering is essential for directing rainwater away from your property. Blocked, damaged or poorly fitted gutters can cause water to overflow, leading to damp walls, stained render and foundation problems.

We install high-capacity uPVC guttering systems designed to handle heavy rainfall, with secure joints and properly positioned downpipes to protect your home.

  • Half-round and square-line profiles
  • High-capacity deep flow systems
  • Matching downpipes and accessories
  • Correct fall for efficient drainage
