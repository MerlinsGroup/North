Home Extensions

Single Storey Extensions

Front and rear extensions designed and delivered. Create the extra space your home needs with a professionally built extension that adds value and transforms how you live.

More Space, Better Living

A single storey extension is one of the most effective ways to add space and value to your home. Whether you need a larger kitchen, an open plan family area, or a dedicated home office, we design and build extensions that work for your lifestyle.

Our team manages the entire project from initial survey through to final handover, coordinating all trades and ensuring the build meets building regulations and your expectations.

  • Rear extensions for kitchens and living spaces
  • Side return extensions to maximise space
  • Front extensions for porches and entrance halls
  • Wrap-around extensions combining multiple aspects

Our Extension Process

Building an extension involves many stages and multiple trades. We coordinate everything so you have one point of contact and one company responsible for delivering your project on time and to budget.

  • Survey & Design – We assess your property and discuss your requirements
  • Planning & Building Regs – We handle applications where required
  • Construction – Groundworks, structure, roofing and weatherproofing
  • Second Fix – Electrics, plumbing, plastering and decoration
  • Handover – Final inspection, snagging and sign-off

Open Plan Kitchen Living

One of the most popular reasons for extending is to create an open plan kitchen and living area. These spaces bring families together and transform how you use your home.

We can incorporate structural openings to connect your new extension to existing rooms, install bifold or sliding doors to flood the space with light, and work with you on the layout to maximise the functionality of your new space.

  • Structural steel beams for open plan layouts
  • Vaulted ceilings and skylights optional
  • Underfloor heating preparation available
  • Coordinated with kitchen suppliers if required
