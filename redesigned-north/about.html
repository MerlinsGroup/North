About North Home Improvements

Yorkshire's trusted home improvement specialists with over 40 years of excellence

Our Story

Founded in 1984, North Home Improvements began as a small family-run business in Huddersfield with a simple mission: to provide Yorkshire homeowners with the highest quality home improvements at fair prices.

Over four decades, we've grown from a team of three to over 50 skilled professionals, including designers, surveyors, installers, and support staff. Despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our core values: quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we're proud to be Yorkshire's leading home improvement company, having transformed thousands of homes across the region.
