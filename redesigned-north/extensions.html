Types of Extensions

Find the perfect extension solution for your home

Single Storey Extensions

Perfect for creating open-plan kitchen-diners, larger living rooms, or additional ground floor space. Often built under permitted development rights without planning permission.

  • Rear extensions
  • Side extensions
  • Wrap-around extensions
  • Kitchen extensions
Get Quote

Double Storey Extensions

Maximize space by building up and out simultaneously. Ideal for adding bedrooms above and living space below, offering excellent value per square foot.

  • Extra bedrooms above
  • Additional bathrooms
  • Living space below
  • Cost-effective solution
Get Quote

Side Return Extensions

Transform wasted side-alley space into usable rooms. Popular in terraced and semi-detached homes for creating larger kitchens or dining areas.

  • Utilize side space
  • Often permitted development
  • Smaller footprint
  • Budget-friendly option
Get Quote

Wrap-Around Extensions

Combine side and rear extensions for maximum space. Creates impressive open-plan living areas perfect for modern family life.

  • Maximum floor space
  • L-shaped design
  • Open-plan living
  • Significant added value
Get Quote

Sun Rooms

A sun room bridges the gap between a traditional extension and a conservatory. With more solid walls than a conservatory but large windows for natural light, they offer year-round comfort.

Perfect for those who want the benefits of a conservatory with better temperature control and the versatility of a conventional extension.

  • Better insulation than conservatories
  • More natural light than extensions
  • Year-round comfort
  • Flexible design options
  • Building regulations compliant
