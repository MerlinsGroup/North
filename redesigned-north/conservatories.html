Luxury Orangeries

The perfect blend of conservatory light and extension solidity

What is an Orangery?

An orangery offers more substantial brick or stone pillars with a flat perimeter roof and a glazed lantern roof in the center. This creates a more room-like feel while still flooding the space with natural light.

Perfect for those who want the benefits of a conservatory but prefer a more solid structure that feels like a natural extension of their home.

  • Brick pillars and perimeter
  • Central roof lantern
  • Better insulation
  • Premium finish
  • Adds significant value
