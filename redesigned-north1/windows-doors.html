Windows & Doors

A rated uPVC windows and doors in modern and traditional styles. High security, energy efficient and professionally installed.

Energy Efficient uPVC Windows

All our double glazed windows are designed to complement your home, with a wide range of beautiful styles to choose from. Our products not only improve the aesthetics but enhance your home's security and energy efficiency.

We supply and install A-rated uPVC windows that help reduce heat loss and lower your energy bills. Available in a variety of colours and finishes to match any property style.

  • Casement windows – the UK's most popular style
  • Tilt and turn windows for easy cleaning
  • Sliding sash windows for period properties
  • Bay and bow windows for added space and light

Composite & uPVC Doors

The look and feel of your home says a lot about you. This is why we offer the perfect selection of external doors in a wide variety of styles, colours and finishes.

Our composite doors combine the traditional appearance of timber with the durability and low maintenance of modern materials. Available with a range of glass designs and hardware options.

  • Composite entrance doors – secure and stylish
  • uPVC panel doors for a classic look
  • French doors opening onto patios and gardens
  • High security multi-point locking systems

Patio & Sliding Doors

Transform your living space with our range of patio and sliding doors. Perfect for connecting your home to the garden and flooding rooms with natural light.

  • Inline sliding patio doors – smooth operation
  • Lift and slide doors for larger openings
  • Low threshold options for easy access
  • Energy efficient double or triple glazing
