About Northants Home Improvements

A dedicated home improvements company providing single storey extensions, uPVC windows and doors, conservatories and roofline services across Northamptonshire.

Experience Built Through Years of Continuous Service

Northants Home Improvements has been delivering projects for homeowners in the region for over 15 years. The company has structured teams for survey, project planning and installation, along with an established network of specialist partners.

This combination supports reliable delivery on both straightforward replacements and more complex schemes. We operate across Northamptonshire and deliver both standard upgrades and higher specification projects in partnership with specialist suppliers.
