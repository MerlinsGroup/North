Aluminium Bifold & Sliding Doors

Aluminium frames offer slimmer sightlines than uPVC, allowing more glass and more light. They're the natural choice for large openings in modern extensions.

  • Slimmer frames than uPVC alternatives
  • Large panel sizes for maximum views
  • Thermally broken for energy efficiency
  • RAL colours and dual-colour options

Media Walls & Bespoke Interiors

A well-designed media wall transforms a living room, providing a focal point that integrates your TV, storage, and sometimes even a fireplace into one cohesive feature.

  • Integrated TV mounting and cable management
  • Electric fireplace integration
  • Hidden storage compartments
  • LED lighting features
